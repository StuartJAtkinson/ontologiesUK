Publisher ontology

Classes · Object properties · Subclasses · Namespaces · Depiction
A model describing the publishing of material by offices within Parliament, for example: the House of Commons Library.

Classes

Agent class

Agents include people and groups, including those outside the Parliamentary Service, participating in parliamentary procedure and contributing to the wider work of Parliament.

Concept class

A SKOS Concept, for example: a library subject term.

Editorial board class

A group overseeing a publisher, for example: the board of POST, made up of Members of Parliament, considering and approving areas of research.

Superclasses · Group

Group class

A group of people. Groups may contain other groups or be empty.

Superclasses · Agent

House class

A parliamentary House, for example: the House of Lords.

Superclasses · Group

Publisher class

An entity responsible for making a resource available.

Superclasses · Agent

Research section class

A group of researchers within the Library of the House of Commons having a particular focus, for example: the Parliament and Constitution Centre.

Superclasses · Publisher

Research service class

A group of researchers, for example: in the Library of the House of Commons, in the Library of the House of Lords or the Parliamentary Office of Science and Technology.

Superclasses · Publisher

Thing having a publisher class

A published thing, which has a publisher.

Object properties

Has editorial board object property

Relates a publisher to its editorial board.

Publisher — Has editorial board — EditorialBoard

Has focus object property

Relates a publisher to one or more policy areas, policy areas being represented as SKOS concepts.

Publisher — Has focus — Concept

Has publisher object property

Relates a thing, which has a publisher, to the publisher of the thing.

ThingHavingPublisher — Has publisher — Publisher

Has research service object property

Relates a House to a research service. A research service may belong to one House or both, for example: the Parliamentary Office of Science and Technology, a bicameral office.

House — Has research service — ResearchService

Has section object property

Relates a research service to a research section within that service.

ResearchService — Has section — ResearchSection
