Treaty ontology

A model describing the form of treaties considered by Parliament, treaty membership of an FCO series and the relationship of a treaty to legislation required to be passed in order for the UK to comply with treaty obligations.

Class Treaty expression

Treaties requiring ratification - including those treaties requiring ratification to which the United Kingdom is acceding - are subject to the Constitutional Reform and Governance Act 2010. The 2010 Act sets out that such treaties must be laid before Parliament prior to ratification and are then subject to a procedure described in part within the Act. All treaties are laid before Parliament following ratification, though this is not a requirement set out in the 2010 Act and does not trigger a parliamentary procedure.

Data property UK coming into force date

The date on which a treaty is recorded as coming into force. Parts of a treaty may come into force on different dates and under differing conditions. Where no single coming into force date is given, coming into force details are given in a coming into force note.
