Common ontology

Classes

# Agent class

Agents include people and groups, including those outside the Parliamentary Service, participating in parliamentary procedure and contributing to the wider work of Parliament.

# Business item class

An item of business conducted either within or outside Parliament. For example: a second reading debate, the tabling of an early day motion, a laying of a paper, the making of a statutory instrument by a government minister.

# Person class

A person.

Superclass: Agent.
