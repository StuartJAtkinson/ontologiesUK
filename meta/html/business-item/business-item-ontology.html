Business item ontology

A model describing items of business taking place in Parliament or outside Parliament - as part of a process involving Parliament - and their scheduling.

Classes

Business item class

An item of business conducted either within or outside Parliament. For example: a second reading debate, the tabling of an early day motion, a laying of a paper or the making of a statutory instrument by a government minister.

Business item series class

A series of regularly recurring business items. For example: questions to the Department of Health and Social Care.

Business item type class

The type of a business item. For example: a committee visit, a committee evidence session or a chamber debate.

Calendared event class

An event during which business items happen.

Document class

A document being a FRBR work.

Place class

A place named in a geography.

Object properties

Calendarisation object property

Describes the process of setting a time or date at which a business item is expected to take place.

Domain: BusinessItem. Range: CalendaredEvent.

Contains business item object property

Allows a business item to be nested inside another business item. For example, a committee meeting during a committee visit.

Domain: BusinessItem. Range: BusinessItem.

Followed by object property

Relates a calendared event to a subsequent calendared event

Domain: CalendaredEvent. Range: CalendaredEvent.

Has business item type object property

Relates a business item to its type.

Domain: BusinessItem. Range: BusinessItemType.

Part of series object property

Relates a business item to a business item series it is part of.

Domain: BusinessItem. Range: BusinessItemSeries.

Record of object property

Relates a document to the calendared event it records.

Domain: Document. Range: CalendaredEvent.

Takes place in object property

Relates a calendared event to the place it occurs.

Domain: CalendaredEvent. Range: Place.

Data properties

Cancelled at data property

The time at which a calendared event was cancelled.

Domain: CalendaredEvent. Range: dateTime.

Disposed of at data property

The time at which an agenda item was completed.

Domain: CalendaredEvent. Range: dateTime.

Not reached data property

A boolean which is true when a calendared event was not reached or became unavailable for disposal.

Domain: CalendaredEvent. Range: boolean.
