Government organisation ontology for UK Parliament

Classes

# AttendsCabinet class

Used to determine if the person incumbent in a government position is able to attend cabinet. Possible values are always, never and when required.

# GovernmentIncumbency class

An incumbency of a person in a government position. This is a specialisation of an incumbency from the agency ontology.

Superclass: Incumbency.

# GovernmentOrganisation class

Government departments, agencies and public bodies which have responsibility for a specific sector of public administration. Is a specialised type of group.

Superclass: Group.

# GovernmentPosition class

A position (job) in a government organisation. Note that not all government positions are in government organisations. e.g. Minister without Portfolio is a position outside any department, agency or public body. This is a specialisation of a position from the agency ontology.

Superclass: Position.

# GovernmentRank class

A packaging of ways to describe the rank of a government position. Different bodies have different ways to describe these.

# Group class

A group of people or a group of groups or a group of people and groups.

# Incumbency class

A position held by a person for a period of time. e.g. Philip Hammond occupying the position of Foreign Secretary between two dates.

# Position class

A position in a group that persists over time which can be occupied by different people at different times e.g. many people have been Foreign Secretary.
