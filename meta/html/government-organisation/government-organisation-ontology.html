Government organisation ontology

Government organisation ontology for UK Parliament

Classes

AttendsCabinet class

Used to determine if the person incumbent in a government position is able to attend cabinet. Possible values are always, never and when required.

GovernmentIncumbency class

An incumbency of a person in a government position. This is a specialisation of an incumbency from the agency ontology.

Incumbency (superclass) → GovernmentIncumbency (class)

GovernmentOrganisation class

Government departments, agencies and public bodies which have responsibility for a specific sector of public administration. Is a specialised type of group.

Group (superclass) → GovernmentOrganisation (class)

GovernmentPosition class

A position (job) in a government organisation. Note that not all government positions are in government organisations. e.g. Minister without Portfolio is a position outside any department, agency or public body. This is a specialisation of a position from the agency ontology.

Position (superclass) → GovernmentPosition (class)

GovernmentRank class

A packaging of ways to describe the rank of a government position. Different bodies have different ways to describe these.

Group class

A group of people or a group of groups or a group of people and groups.

Incumbency class

A position held by a person for a period of time. e.g. Philip Hammond occupying the position of Foreign Secretary between two dates.

Position class

A position in a group that persists over time which can be occupied by different people at different times e.g. many people have been Foreign Secretary.

Data properties

cabinetRank data property

Used to capture how the government ranks assorted positions in the cabinet. e.g. whether the Foreign Secretary is ranked above or below the Health Secretary.

GovernmentPosition (domain) → cabinetRank → integer (range)

clerksRank data property

GovernmentRank (domain) → clerksRank → langString (range)

governmentSortOrder data property

Used to determine how the government chooses to order government organisations. e.g. whether they choose to rank the Foreign Office higher than the Department of Health.

GovernmentOrganisation (domain) → governmentSortOrder → integer (range)

isLayingPosition data property

Used to determine if a government position has the authority to lay papers into Parliament.

GovernmentPosition (domain) → isLayingPosition → boolean (range)

isPaid data property

Used to describe whether an incumbency is paid or not.

GovernmentIncumbency (domain) → isPaid → boolean (range)

ministerialRank data property

GovernmentRank (domain) → ministerialRank → integer (range)

oppositionSortOrder data property

Used to determine how the official opposition chooses to order government organisations. e.g. whether they choose to rank the Foreign Office higher than the Department of Health.

GovernmentOrganisation (domain) → oppositionSortOrder → integer (range)

orderRank data property

GovernmentRank (domain) → orderRank → integer (range)

paidToAttendCabinet data property

Used to determine whether a government position is paid to attend cabinet or not.

GovernmentPosition (domain) → paidToAttendCabinet → boolean (range)

statsRank data property

GovernmentRank (domain) → statsRank → langString (range)
