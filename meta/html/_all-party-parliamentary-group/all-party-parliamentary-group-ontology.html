BenefitInKind class

A benefit in kind such as goods or services received by the group where the total value of the benefit from that source exceeds £1500 and the benefit was received on or after the date of the group’s Inaugural Meeting.

Superclasses · RegisteredBenefit

FinancialBenefit class

A financial benefit received by the group where the total value of the benefit from that source exceeds £1500 and the benefit was received on or after the date of the group’s Inaugural Meeting.

Superclasses · RegisteredBenefit

RegisteredBenefit class

A benefit received by the group from a source other than Parliament where the total value of the benefit from that source exceeds £1500 and the benefit was received on or after the date of the group’s Inaugural Meeting .

StatusType class

Status of an APPG, for example, registered, pending.
