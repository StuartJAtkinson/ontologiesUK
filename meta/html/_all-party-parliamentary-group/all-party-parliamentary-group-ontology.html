Classes

Agent class

An agent. The superset of all people and all groups of people.

All Party Parliamentary Group class

All Party Parliamentary Group.

Group (superclass) → All Party Parliamentary Group (class)

All Party Parliamentary Group Category class

A category of APPG, for example one focussed on a country or on a subject area.

BenefitInKind class

A benefit in kind such as goods or services received by the group where the total value of the benefit from that source exceeds £1500 and the benefit was received on or after the date of the group’s Inaugural Meeting.

RegisteredBenefit (superclass) → BenefitInKind (class)

FinancialBenefit class

A financial benefit received by the group where the total value of the benefit from that source exceeds £1500 and the benefit was received on or after the date of the group’s Inaugural Meeting.

RegisteredBenefit (superclass) → FinancialBenefit (class)

FinancialReportingYear class

The year for which an APPG makes its financial report.

Group class

A group of people or a group of groups or a group of people and groups.

Member class

A current or former Member of a House.

RegisteredBenefit class

A benefit received by the group from a source other than Parliament where the total value of the benefit from that source exceeds £1500 and the benefit was received on or after the date of the group’s Inaugural Meeting .

StatusType class

Status of an APPG, for example, registered, pending.

Object properties

hasAllPartyParliamentaryGroupCategory object property

Relates an APPG to its category, such as a country or subject related APPG.

Domain: APPG
Range: APPGCategory

hasPublicEnquiryPoint object property

Relates an APPG to an secondary enquiry point for that APPG. This may be a contact within or outside Parliament.

Domain: APPG
Range: Agent

hasRegisteredContact object property

Relates an APPG to its registered contact. This is normally the Chair of the APPG, therefore a member.

Domain: APPG
Range: Member

hasSecretariatRole object property

Relates an external group to an APPG where they are providing secretariat (administrative) services to that APPG.

Domain: Group
Range: APPG

hasStatusType object property

Relates an All Party ParliamentaryGroup to its status type.

Domain: APPG
Range: StatusType

receivedBenefit object property

Relates an APPG to a benefit received.

Domain: APPG
Range: RegisteredBenefit

reportedIn object property

Relates a registered benefit to the financial reporting year it falls into.

Domain: RegisteredBenefit
Range: FinancialReportingYear
