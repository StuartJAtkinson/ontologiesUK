Geographic area ontology

Classes · Object properties · Data properties · Subclasses · Namespaces · Depiction
A model describing geographic areas in the UK - being constituency areas, local authority areas, countries, and statistical regions within England - and the containment relationships between such areas.

Boundary set

A boundary set for UK Parliament constituency areas, as proposed in a boundary review by the Boundary Commission and established by statute.

Combined authority area

Class

The geographic area in which a combined authority may exercise its powers. A combined authority may be created in England under powers set out in the Local Democracy, Economic Development and Construction Act 2009.

Partly contained by

Object property

Relates a geographic area to a geographic area it is partly contained by. A constituency area may be partly contained by a lower tier local authority area, by an upper tier local authority area, or by neither. A lower tier local authority area or an upper tier local authority area may be partly contained by a constituency area. The Local Democracy, Economic Development and Construction Act 2009 does not preclude an upper tier local authority area being partly contained by a combined authority area. As at November 2021, no upper tier local authority area is partly contained by a combined authority area.

GeographicArea — Partly contained by — GeographicArea

Wholly contained by

Object property

Relates a geographic area to the geographic area it is wholly contained by. A constituency area may be wholly contained by a lower tier local authority area, by an upper tier local authority area, or by neither. A constituency area in England is wholly contained by an English region. A constituency area is wholly contained by a country. A lower tier local authority area may be wholly contained by a constituency area. A lower tier local authority area is wholly contained by an upper tier local authority area, except where the lower tier local authority area is also the upper tier local authority area. An upper tier local authority area may be wholly contained by a constituency area. As at November 2021, an upper tier local authority area is wholly contained by a combined authority area, where a combined authority area exists.An upper tier local authority area in England is wholly contained by an English region. An upper tier local authority area is wholly contained by a country. A combined authority area may be wholly contained by an English region. A combined authority area is wholly contained by the country of England. An English region is wholly contained by the country of England.
