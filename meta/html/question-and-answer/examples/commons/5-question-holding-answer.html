Question and answer ontology

Classes · Object properties · Namespaces · Depiction
Question and answer ontology for UK Parliament. This model only deals with questions that have been successfully tabled or raised as urgent, private notice or business questions in either House. Note that the handling of questions as calendar business events, any ministerial statements acting as corrections to answers and tabling events will be dealt with elsewhere.
