Description

A model describing the laying of papers into a House and the authorities those papers are laid under.

Laying authority c

IRI http://parliament.uk/ontologies/laying/LayingAuthority
Is Defined By pl:
Description

The authority under which a paper is laid. The authority for laying may cite a section of legislation, standing order or resolution giving authorisation for that laying.
In Domain Of pl:hasLayingAuthorityType op
In Range Of pl:hasLayingAuthority op

Laying authority type c

IRI http://parliament.uk/ontologies/laying/LayingAuthorityType
Is Defined By pl:
Description

The type of an authority under which a paper is laid. A paper may be laid under prerogative powers and said to be laid 'by command'. A paper laid in order to comply with legislation may be said to be laid 'by Act'. A paper may be laid in order to comply with standing orders, or with an opposed or unopposed return motion.
In Domain Of pl:parentLayingAuthorityType op
In Range Of
  • pl:hasLayingAuthorityType op
  • pl:parentLayingAuthorityType op
