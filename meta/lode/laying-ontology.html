Description

A model describing the laying of papers before a House and the authority a paper is laid under.

Laying authority type c

IRI http://parliament.uk/ontologies/laying/LayingAuthorityType
Is Defined By pl:
Description

The type of an authority under which a paper is laid before a House. Legislation may impose a duty on a body to lay papers before a House, these are laid 'by Act'. Command papers are laid in a House by the government and are laid 'by Command'.
