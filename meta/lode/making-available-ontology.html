Description

A model describing the act of making material available to a House and any subsequent withdrawal.

Classes

Session c

IRI http://parliament.uk/ontologies/making-available/Session
Is Defined By pma:
Description

A period during which Parliament is able to conduct business on days of its choosing. A session is within a Parliament period. The first session of a Parliament begins on the same day as the Parliament period and ends on a day named in a subsequent prorogation proclamation. Subsequent sessions within the Parliament period begin and end on dates named in subsequent prorogation proclamations. The final session of a Parliament ends either with prorogation followed by dissolution or with dissolution. Session periods are the same for both Houses.
In Range Of
  • pma:duringSession op
  • pma:intoSession op

Object Properties

During session op

IRI http://parliament.uk/ontologies/making-available/duringSession
Is Defined By pma:
Description

Relates an act of making a thing available to the session during which that thing is made available. A thing made available during prorogation is during no session. An act of making available is during one session, or none.
Domain pma:MakingAvailable c
Range pma:Session c

Into session op

IRI http://parliament.uk/ontologies/making-available/intoSession
Is Defined By pma:
Description

Relates an act of making a thing available to the session into which that thing is made available. A thing made available during a prorogation is considered to be made available into the following session. An act of making available is into one session.
Domain pma:MakingAvailable c
Range pma:Session c

Made available by op

IRI http://parliament.uk/ontologies/making-available/madeAvailableBy
Is Defined By pma:
Description

Relates an act of making a thing available to the incumbency of the person in a position who makes that thing available, for example: the depositing of a paper by Philip Hammond as Foreign Secretary. An act of making available is by one person incumbent in one position.
